About the System

The BSIT Attendance Monitoring System is designed specifically for Our Lady of Fatima University's Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program. This web-based application streamlines the process of tracking student attendance, making it easier for faculty and administrators to maintain accurate records.

Built with modern web technologies, the system offers a user-friendly interface that requires minimal training. Whether you're marking daily attendance or generating semester reports, our system provides the tools you need.
